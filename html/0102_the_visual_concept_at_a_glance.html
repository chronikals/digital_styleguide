The visual concept at a glance

The ABB bar – a visual landmark

Provides consistency and familiarity across ABB’s software interfaces, and typically contains main navigation and critical information.

Flexible and space efficient layout sections

Colored backgrounds are used to define different sections of the interface. These can be combined in many ways.

Minimalistic and intuitive controls

Subtle effects support user flow and interaction.

Emphasized dynamic information

The use of color makes dynamic information stand out.

Fresh and clean colors

Colors are used sparingly to communicate meaning, convey visual differentiation and provide consistency.

Rational typography: Helvetica Neue eText

A systematic and precise typeface with good legibility and a clear link to our brand identity.

Consistent tone of voice

Text is written in a language that is clear, precise and free of jargon.

Simple and clear icons

Icons follow the ABB icon philosophy, optimized for software interfaces.
