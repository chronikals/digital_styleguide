Design protection

Protecting the visual design of your application

The design system has been protected through a general design registration in DM/087 951 and DM/087 952, which have been filed in CH, EU, JP, KR, NO, SG and US.

The recommendation is for each product to consider obtaining design protection on the distinctive features of the product-specific visual designs. Contact your local IPSO representative or patent@se.abb.com to receive guidance on how to protect your product.
