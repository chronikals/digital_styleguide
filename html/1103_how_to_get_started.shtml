How to get started

1Contact the UI/UX design responsible within your organization. If there is nobody within your organization or BU, contact the CommonUX team directly to get startup guidance.

2Decide on appropriate process for your case together with designer. The process will vary according to:

  • ambition level
  • development process
  • scope of development project (new product, new feature or redesign of existing product)

3Get designs reviewed by CommonUX team before major implementation direction is set, and your application reviewed after implementation.
