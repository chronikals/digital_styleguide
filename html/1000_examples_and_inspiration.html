Examples

This section contains a set of example screens from a variety of ABB software interfaces. These applications have been pilots to test, evaluate and refine the graphical principles in the style guide. The screens presented are snap-shots from this “proof of concept” process.

The designs are not to be considered as perfect and refined examples to be followed in detail, but rather to be used as inspiration.
