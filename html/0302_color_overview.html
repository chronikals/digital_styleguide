Primary colors for dark interfaces

The highlighted colors should be your primary choice for the main components when creating a dark interface.

Full range

The full range of blue and grey shades shown here are the foundation of all components found in this style guide. They are used for backgrounds, details and controls in both light and dark interfaces. The lighter part of the blue scale is used as accent colors, for highlighting information, selected items and in data visualizations.
