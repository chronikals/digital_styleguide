About this style guide

This document describes the ABB look & feel for software products. Many of our customers and end-users see ABB primarily through the day-to-day interactions with the software products we deliver. Providing professional, modern and harmonized user interfaces is a basic requirement in today’s market.

This style guide is valid for all types of software from all divisions and business units within ABB. The primary target applications are those that are either sold to customers or delivered to customers as part of service agreements. Other applications, such as engineering tools or internal tools are also free to follow this style guide.

This style guide has been produced by the Common UX project, a part of ABB’s cross-division SW2020 initiative. For more information, please visit
