Design principles

1Give clarity to complex information through a simple and rational framework

2Support user work flow by offering easy to understand components and navigation

3Focus on clear presentation of dynamic information.

The design principles are based on established usability practices:

  • Simplicity
  • Match between system and the real world
  • Consistency
  • Minimizing cognitive load
  • Efficient interactions
  • Forgiveness
  • Good system feedback
  • Effective use of language
  • Effective information presentation
  • Preservation of context
