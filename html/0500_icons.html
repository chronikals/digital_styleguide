Sizes and formats

Icons are symbols that represent an object, a concept, a service or an action. They provide information quickly and may enhance usability if used appropriately. Do not use icons as aesthetic decoration, but rather for communication.

All icons used in ABB interfaces should be based on the same design principles to ensure recognition, consistent brand appearance and optimized navigation/orientation.

A selection of icons from the ABB icon library

Sizes and formats

The ABB icon library contains icons in 3 sizes, to accommodate different displays and contexts of use:

Small - 16x16 px

For use on, and in relation to, standard buttons and controls.

Medium - 20x20 px

For use in the top bar.

Large - 24x24 px

Typically used on mobile and high-resolution displays.

Scaling icons

Sometimes larger icons are needed, for example for illustrative purposes. A common misconception is that vector icons, like SVG, can be freely scaled to any size and still maintain sharpness. However, to always ensure pixel perfection and legibility on any screen resolution, icons must be scaled in multiples of the basic icon sizes (@2x, @3x, @4x etc.).

Large icons are optimized for use on mobile and high-resolution displays, and can be upscaled @2x (ex. iPhone 4, 5 and 6) or @3x (ex. iPhone 6 Plus) to adapt to various platforms and screens. These displays have a higher pixel density and require images with more pixels.
