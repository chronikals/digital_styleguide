Progress indicators

Progress indicators provide immediate feedback to users that their actions have started and reduces frustration in long operations.

Progress indicators should be used for determinate processes while spinners should be used when there is no reasonably accurate indication of how much time or effort is left.

Consider following platform conventions whenever applicable.
